Data Description: Factors

  • Gender (Binary). Male/Female
  • Shielded (Quarantined). Yes/No
  • Athlete/Non-Athlete
  • Marital Status 5-level
  • Age group 7-level & ordered.
  • Country during lockdown. 7-level.
  • Smoking status. 7-level.
  • Week Social Distancing. 7-level & ordered.
  • # in Lockdown bubble. 7-level & ordered.

Data Description: Numeric

  • Hours Sleep. Theoretical: >0 hours / Realized: 1.5-10.5.
  • Athlete Identification Index (AIMS). Th & Re: 1-7.*
  • Anxiety & Depression Scale (HADS). Th: 0-42 / Re: 0-36*.
  • Brief Resilience Scale (RES). Th: 0-30 /Re: 6-30*
  • Loneliness Scale (LONE) Th & Re: 0-6*
  • Response: MHC-SF. Represents overall well-being. Th: 0-84 / Re: 2-70 *

* Theoretical/Realized Range

Main Research Questions

  • Were athletes and non-athletes affected from COVID-19 lockdown/social distancing in the same way?
  • Are there sex-based differences in the effects of COVID-19 on mental health?
  • Under controlled demographic information, what is the relationship between MH-SF index and other sub-indices in this survey?
  • Are there additional variables having strong relationship with MH-SF index?

ANCOVA Model

After the model selection procedure, we set

\[\begin{align*} y_{i} = &\mu + \beta_{1}I_{gender=2}(x_i) + \sum_{j=2}^7\beta_{2j}I_{\text{age grp}=j}(x_{2i}) \\ &+ \sum_{j=1}^7 \beta_{9_j}I_{\text{wk soc dist}=j}(x_{9i}) + \beta_10 I_{\text{athlete}=2}(x_{10i}) \\ &+ \beta_{13} x_{13i} + \beta_{15} x_{15i} + \epsilon_i \\ \end{align*}\]

where \(\epsilon ~ N(0,\sigma^2)\), and

  • \(I_A\)s are indicator variables (\(I_A(x)= 1\) if \(x \in A\), or \(0\) otherwise)
  • \(x_{13i}\): HADS of i-th point
  • \(x_{15i}\): LONE

Grouped Lasso Model

\[\begin{align*} y_{i} = &\mu + \beta_{1}I_{gender=2}(x_i) + \sum_{j=2}^7\beta_{2j}I_{\text{age grp}=j}(x_{2i}) + \sum_{j=2}^7\beta_{3j}I_{\text{country lockdown}=j}(x_{3i}) \\ &+ \sum_{j=2}^5\beta_{4j}I_{\text{marital}=j}(x_{4i}) + \sum_{j=2}^7\beta_{5j}I_{\text{smoking}=j}(x_{5i}) + \beta_{6}I_{\text{fruits}=2}(x_{6i}) \\ &+ \beta_{7} x_{7i} + \beta_{8}I_{\text{shielded}=2}(x_{8i}) + \sum_{j=1}^7 \beta_{9_j}I_{\text{wk soc dist}=j}(x_{9i}) + \beta_{10} I_{\text{athlete}=2}(x_{10i}) \\ &+ \beta_{11} x_{11i} + \beta_{12} x_{12i} + \beta_{13} x_{13i} + \beta_{14} x_{14i} + \beta_{15} x_{15i} + \epsilon_i \\ \end{align*}\]

where

  • \(I_A\)s are indicator variables, (\(I_A(x)= 1\) if \(x \in A\), or \(0\) otherwise)
  • \(x_{7i}\): hour_sleep of the i-th observation
  • Likewise, \(x_{11i}\): lockdown_bubble, \(x_{12i}\): AIMS, \(x_{13i}\): HADS, \(x_{14i}\): RES, and \(x_{15i}\): LONE

with an error term \(\epsilon_i\).

Grouped Lasso: Fitting

  • Setting each category and each continuous variable to its own group.
  • Notation
    • \(\mathbf{X}^{(g)} = (\mathbf{X}_{g1}, \mathbf{X}_{g2}, ..., \mathbf{X}_{gp_g})\)
    • \(\beta^{(g)} = (\beta_{g1}, \beta_{g2}, ..., \beta_{gp_g})^T.\)

Then our objective function for grouped LASSO is: \[ \big | \big|y-\sum_{g=1}^G \mathbf{X}^{(g)}\beta^{(g)} \big |\big|_2^2 + \lambda \sum_{g=1}^G \sqrt{p_g}||\beta^{(g)}||_{2}\]
